Welcome!

I am Baoquan Zhang, a Ph.D. Candidate (Sep.2015 - current) in Computer Science at University of Minnesota Twin-Cities advised by Professor David H.C. Du. Currently, I am a member of CRIS (Center for Research in Intelligent Storage), UMN and working on Memory/Storage Systems, Key-Value Stores, RAID Systems and etc..

Before joining UMN, I got B.E. (Sep. 2008 - July 2012) and M.S. (Sep. 2012 - April 2015) degrees in Computer Science at Harbin Engineering University, China. As a Research Intern (June 2013 - April 2015), I worked in Cloud Storage and Distributed Systems in the storage system group at Research Institute of Information Technology, Tsinghua University, Beijing advised by Prof. Dongsheng Wang.
